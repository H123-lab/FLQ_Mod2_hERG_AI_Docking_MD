Docking Protocol for hERG Channel Interaction Analysis

Protein Target
--------------
Human Ether-à-go-go Related Gene (hERG) potassium channel

PDB ID:
5VA1

Protein Preparation
-------------------
The hERG channel structure (PDB ID: 5VA1) was obtained from the Protein Data Bank.
Protein preparation involved removal of crystallographic water molecules,
addition of polar hydrogens, and assignment of appropriate partial charges.

Ligand Preparation
------------------
Fluoroquinolone analogs were prepared using ChemDraw and converted to 3D
structures using Open Babel. Energy minimization was performed prior to docking.

Docking Software
----------------
CB-Dock2 (AutoDock Vina based docking engine)

Docking Parameters
------------------
Search space was automatically detected using cavity detection in CB-Dock2.

Number of docking poses: 10
Exhaustiveness: default
Scoring function: AutoDock Vina

Key Binding Site Residues
-------------------------
Tyr542
Phe551
Ala548
Arg541

These residues are known contributors to hERG channel drug binding
and cardiotoxicity through aromatic and hydrophobic interactions.

Docking Output
--------------
Representative docking pose for FLQ_Mod_2 was selected based on
binding energy and orientation relative to key residues.

Predicted docking score:
FLQ_Mod_2 ≈ −8.1 kcal/mol
